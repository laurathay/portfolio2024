Fo Sho Bro

One of the best looking IG style coffee shop. Divided by the entrance with the pool, then the lounge and a backyard with another restaurant.

The spot is okay expensive, I paid like 150 baht for a drink and 250 baht for a quesadillas con queso. A lots of locals coming to take pictures too and its funny how they don't care about everybody taking a picture on every corner of the coffeeshop.
